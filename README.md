# CPI-Inflation-case-study
Analysed 2013–2023 CPI data from GOI; identified Food (~45%) as largest inflation driver.
Detected peak YoY inflation of 6.62% in 2022 (COVID impact on food & healthcare).
Evaluated monthly food inflation trends (Jun’22–May’23); studied COVID impact on CPI pre/post Mar’20.
Correlated oil prices (2021–23) with transport inflation (r=0.96).
Tech Stack: Excel (Pivot, Formulas, Charts, Correlation)
